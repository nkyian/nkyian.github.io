About

So what does bring you here? Searching for Elixir tips & tricks, latest news on Actor-model programming? Just interested learning together with me, and exploring wast fields of software development? Then you’re welcome!

My name is Norbert, I’m a 23 year old software developer that’s highly interested in writing maintainable, highly distributed and efficient software, also as you can see writing simple and easy to understand blog posts about software development.

I’ve worked on many different fields of development, starting from writing UWP applications for Windows tablets; using keras LSTM networks to predict future time-series data; writing highly distributed software for IoT startups and lastly writing code for microcontrollers. So that’s a pretty broad set of skills that I have acquired over the past 4 years.

Here we’ll mostly explore actor-model and how to use it when writing software with Akka and erlang/elixir (OTP) libraries/languages. Of course it’s not gonna be about actor-model only you’ll find blog posts about latest news and tips on scala, elixir and some go inbetween.

Happy reading!
